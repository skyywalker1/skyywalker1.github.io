Delicious Recipes from the Farm

Homemade Goat Milk Soap Scrub

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fine sugar
  • 1/2 cup goat milk soap, grated
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • Essential oil of your choice

Instructions: Mix all ingredients until well combined. Store in an airtight jar. Use as a gentle exfoliating scrub.

DIY Air Freshener Spray

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup distilled water
  • 2 tbsp vodka
  • 15 drops essential oil (lavender, lemon, or pine)

Instructions: Mix ingredients in a spray bottle. Shake well before each use and spritz around your home for a natural fragrance.

Carpet Freshener Powder

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup baking soda
  • 15 drops essential oil (optional)

Instructions: Mix together and sprinkle on carpets. Let sit for 15-20 minutes, then vacuum thoroughly for a fresh scent.
